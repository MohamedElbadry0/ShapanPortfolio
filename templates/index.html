ABOUT ME

WHO I AM

Video Editor

I’m Ahmed Shapan — a professional video editor with over 4 years of experience in crafting cinematic, high-performance content for YouTube and commercial platforms. Whether it’s short-form reels or long-format storytelling, I specialize in turning raw footage into dynamic visuals that captivate and convert. From creators to brands, I help bring visions to life with precision, pace, and purpose. .

Film Maker

I approach every edit like a filmmaker: storytelling first, aesthetics second. I believe great edits don’t just look good — they move people. Whether it’s a 60-second short or a 6-minute doc, I cut with intention, rhythm, and a cinematic eye.

I began editing in 2020 during the pandemic — what started as a creative outlet quickly became a full-blown passion. Over the past few years, I’ve worked with clients from all over the world, including the UK, Italy, China, and beyond. From YouTube creators and agencies to independent filmmakers and business owners, I’ve helped shape stories through sharp, engaging, and cinematic edits. No matter the project, I bring precision, energy, and a storyteller’s mindset to every frame.
